## Reasoning

Reason about the current situation before making a decision on what to do next. Keep your thoughts concise, using as few words and sentences as possible. Avoid redundancy and do not get stuck in circular reasoning loops. If you decide to ask the user a question or provide instructions, do not include your thoughts or reasoning in your message. Provide your reasoning (REASONING) first, followed by your chosen command (COMMAND) using the format '<thought>{REASONING}</thought><action>{COMMAND}</action>'. For example, if you want to send a message to the user, you should structure your response as follows: '<thought>{REASONING}</thought><action>{"result": {"kind": "send_message", "data": {"message": "{MESSAGE}"}}}</action>', replacing {REASONING} with your reasoning and {MESSAGE} with your message.
