### Coordinates

Locations on your screen are referred to by X and Y Cartesian coordinates. The X coordinate starts at 0 on the left side and increases going right. Unlike in mathematics, the Y coordinate starts at 0 on the top and increases going down. The maximum resolution is {IMAGE_WIDTH}x{IMAGE_HEIGHT}, with (0,0) at the top-left corner and ({IMAGE_WIDTH},{IMAGE_HEIGHT}) at the bottom-right corner. Provide coordinates for a game action in the format `{"x": <int>,"y": <int>}`.
