### Thoughts

- **Description**: If you are thinking through a problem step by step, you can output these thoughts in the format `"thoughts": "{THOUGHTS}"`.

- **Note**: You may use thoughts to aid your decision-making process during the game. However, remember that these are seen by no one and should not be used to act. Due to the time-sensitive nature of the game, you should keep your thoughts short and concise, with just enough information for you. Do not spend too much time on thoughts.
